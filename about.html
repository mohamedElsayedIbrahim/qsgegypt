Who Are We?

A private Egyptian Joint Stock company established in 1997 with a current paid in capital of EGP 2 million, and is wholly owned and operated by Egyptian nationals.

The company’s scope of work includes the following activities :

Inspection & Expediting Services

Asset Valuation & Surveying

Oil, Gas, & Power Services (ASME & TPI)

Feasibility Studies & Consulting Services

To carry out its work, QSG relies on a selected group of experts with extensive technical expertise, gained through broad experience inside Egypt and internationally.

The company holds the ISO 9001:2015 certificate since 1999 and is renewed periodically , providing evidence of our ongoing conformity to quality management system requirements.

Quality Services Group (QSG)

The company’s workforce includes 27 experts, consultants, and specialists at the head office in Cairo and the Alexandria branch.

Mission

Is to provide our clients with the highest level of performance, quality and outstanding services and to fulfill their expectations , and guarantee their satisfaction .

Work Methodology

  • Full commitment to international professional standards.
  • Providing continuous training for our team to enhance their technical and managerial skills.
  • • Expand our portfolio to meet evolving client needs.
  • Encouraging and motivating our staff to foster creativity, innovation, and teamwork.

Objectives

To achieve a strong presence in the Egyptian and international markets as a leading Egyptian company providing third-party services in site surveys, inspection, assets valuation and consulting services.

This is achieved by

  • Obtaining international accreditation for asset valuation activities.
  • Developing solid relations with our agents worldwide.
  • Enhancing performance by leveraging modern technologies.
  • Providing ASME services in Egypt and across the globe .
